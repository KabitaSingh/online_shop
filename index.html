Easy Omelet(Omelette)

Ingredients:
  • 2 to 3 large eggs
  • pinch of salt
  • pinch of pepper
  • 2 tsp. of water(optional)
  • cheese
  • tomato
  • green onion
  • bacon(or ham,sausage)
Steps:
  1. Mix egges with salt, pepper using fork
  2. Heat a pan in medium flame and use a little bit of oil
  3. Pour the eggs into the pan
  4. Add topping like:cheese,tomato,green onion and bacon
  5. Finally fold the omelet and serve it warm
